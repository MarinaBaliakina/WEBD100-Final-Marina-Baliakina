Oranged

Oranged, is a young electronic music artist originally from Russia and now based in Toronto, Canada.

Coming from Russia, Oranged went through many experiences that transformed his life in a way that wasn't planned. Oranged real name is Andrei. Andrei started creating music at a young age, 9 years old. Now Andrei is 17 and he explores various topics in his art, such as complicated emotions, life struggles, and maturing.

Oranged's music is characterized by a unique blend of heartfelt melodies and innovative beats, reflecting his journey and inner world. Drawing inspiration from personal challenges and the vibrant cultural influences of his new home in Toronto, Andrei channels his thoughts and feelings into powerful electronic compositions. Each track tells a story, offering listeners a glimpse into his evolving perspective on life, growth, and self-discovery.
